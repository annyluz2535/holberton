    chocoana identity

    The foundation of our work is the defense of the territory and the ethnic-cultural recognition. The territoriality of the black communities of the Medio Atrato has to do with the history that we have lived, with the culture of our people, which is reflected in the traditions and customs that have allowed us to develop as an ethnic group. All of this is rooted in traditional knowledge, which for us represents what we have been learning from generation to generation and what our elders have taught us. That is why we say that for us the territory includes much more than the place where we build the house and where we have the crops. The territory includes for us the river, the swamps, the forests and in general all the resources, since it is there where we develop our lives. What has defined property in the communities is based on tradition and inheritance, by virtue of which the property of the territory is ancestral.
